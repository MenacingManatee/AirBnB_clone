My House

2 Guests

2 Bedrooms

1.5 Bathroom(s)

Owner: Jake Fakenamington

From the outside this house looks warm and cozy. It has been built with white cedar wood and has marble decorations. Tall, half rounded windows add to the overall look of the house and have been added to the house in a fairly asymmetrical pattern. The building is fairly rounded in shape. The house is half surrounded by stylish gardens. The second floor is smaller than the first, which allowed for a roof garden on two sides of the house. This floor has roughly the same style as the floor below.

Tiny House

0 Guests

1 Bedrooms

1 Bathroom(s)

Owner: Ollie Tabooger

From the outside this house looks snug and comfortable. It has been built with burgandy bricks and has granite decorations. Tall, rounded windows allow enough light to enter the home and have been added to the house in a mostly asymmetric way. The roof is high and square shaped and is covered with seagrass. One small chimney sits at the side of the house. Several long, thin windows let in plenty of light to the rooms below the roof.

A Suite

3 Guests

4 Bedrooms

3 Bathroom(s)

Owner: Yerdas Selzavon

From the outside this house looks lavish. It has been built with bricks covered in render and has tan stone decorations. Tall, large windows allow enough light to enter the home and have been added to the house in a very asymmetric way. The building is shaped like a short U. The two extensions are linked by a covered patio. The second floor is smaller than the first, which creates a layered style of look in combination with the roof. This floor has a very different style than the floor below.
